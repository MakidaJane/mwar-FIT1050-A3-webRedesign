OUR PROGRAMS

COME N' TRY

Are you curious about trying archery, but aren’t sure where to start? Join our ‘Come N Try’ program to get a feel for the sport! All equipment will be provided, a long with instruction from a trained coach.

LEARN TO SHOOT

Book a ‘Learn to Shoot’ session to be taught all the basics of archery, from stance to bowstrings! This program welcomes both beginners and trained archers.

SHOOT TOGETHER

Our club is perfect for group events. Whether a party or a team-building exercise, we’re happy to accommodate you. Check out our ‘Shoot Together’ program to see our options and availability!

CURRENT FUNDRAISER

At everdeen Archers we are passionate about giving back to the community. From mental health, kids education and cancer we have donated to may causes. See what fundraiser is on now!
